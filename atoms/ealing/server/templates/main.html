Before lockdown

The commuter area of Ealing has a similar spread of outlets as Sale, with health and beauty and restaurants being popular in the area

Lockdown: 22% open

During lockdown, 22% of its outlets remained open. This is mostly because of a high number of food shops and chemists

After lockdown: 88% open

But once non-essential retail opened, the area enjoyed a reopening rate of 88%. 69 health and beauty shops opened alone, the most common outlet on the high street
