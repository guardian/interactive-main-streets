Before lockdown

The mix of outlets on Sale’s streets is more diverse than Spinningfields, with hairdressers, fast food and household supplies being more common.

Lockdown: 25% open

These outlets did close as lockdown forced hundreds of Sale’s shops to shut

After lockdown: 88% open

But, once restrictions were lifted, all of these outlets saw reopening rates at around 90%.

Even with its fashion, charity and transport outlets suffering lower reopening rates, Sale’s high street was faring much better than Spinningfields’ in July
